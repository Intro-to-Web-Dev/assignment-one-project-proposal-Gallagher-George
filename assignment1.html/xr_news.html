Mission Statement

XR Nuntium is here to bring to you the reader the best information on the latest technological advances and popular articles in virtual and augmented reality.
XR is an umbrella term for anything VR, AR and any other sort of mixed reality, including our own true reality.
This is possible by writing and sharing articles and information from trusted sources, experienced writers, and subject matter experts.
We do this in hopes to grow and properly inform the quickly developing Virtual and Augmented reality communities and businesses that wish to join us in our dive into the future of these new virtual worlds.

Goals for this Site

  • Inform the public on how virtual reality will effect our lives
  • Create and grow community of XR enthusiasts
  • Make XR Nuntium a go-to site for all things XR.
